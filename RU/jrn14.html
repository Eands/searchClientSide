Теоретическая физика

С.В. Белим. Влияние конкуренции между дальнодействием и близкодействием на критическое поведение неупорядоченных сжимаемых систем.
The renormalization-group method is applied to analysis of phase transitions in systems with long-range interaction where the order parametr is coupled to nonordering additional elastic variable. A variety of critical and tricritical behavior is found as function of the physical variables and possible macroccopic constraints imposed on the system. The critical and the tricritical exponents were calculated in two-loop order with using Pade-Borel summation technique.

Solution of such type for Dirac equation with stationary \(\delta\)-functional electro-magnetic potential are found. It is shown that ghost spinous can be localized by such field.
